Oxygenation Evolution over the Days of the Month

Tips According to Oxygenation Level:

  • 95-100%: Normal oxygen level. No concerns. Continue with your usual activities and stay hydrated.

  • 91-94%: Slight decrease in oxygen levels. It may be helpful to rest and get fresh air. If levels remain low, it is advisable to consult a healthcare professional.

  • Below 90%: Hypoxemia. Low oxygen level that may indicate a significant health issue. Immediate consultation with a doctor or a visit to a healthcare facility is necessary.
